ABOUT JY MEC

JY MEC is the Jesus Youth branch of Govt. Model Engineering College Kochi. Started with an aim of challenging young people to live a meaningful, creative and fulfilling life, we are a warm and welcoming community of MECians & X-MECians

OUR GATHERINGS

We conduct monthly gatherings where the members come together to spend some time for the Lord and for themselves. There would be sharings, talks, fun activities, games etc for the members to de-stress themselves and to find some calm in the busy modern life
